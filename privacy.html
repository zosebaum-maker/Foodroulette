Privacy Policy

Ric's Food Roulette

Overview

Ric's Food Roulette is a restaurant discovery app that helps you decide where to eat. This policy explains what information is used when you use the app.

Location Data

Ric's Food Roulette requests access to your device's location to find restaurants near you. Your location is:

  • Used only to search for nearby restaurants
  • Sent to our server to query the Google Places API
  • Rounded to approximately 1km precision before being sent
  • Never stored, logged, or shared with third parties beyond Google Maps Platform
  • Never used for advertising or tracking purposes

If you decline location access, the app uses a default set of restaurants and no location data is collected.

Restaurant Data

Restaurant information (names, addresses, ratings, photos, hours) is retrieved from the Google Maps Platform Places API and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store or sell this data.

Search results are temporarily cached in your browser's local storage (localStorage) for up to 1 hour to reduce API calls. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

No Accounts or Tracking

Ric's Food Roulette does not require an account. We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking tools. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.

Spin History

Your recent spin history is stored in your browser's local storage only. It stays on your device and is cleared when you clear your browser data. We never see or access this information.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps Platform — restaurant data and photos. Google Privacy Policy · Google Maps Terms
  • DoorDash — optional food ordering links open in a new tab. DoorDash Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google's servers. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Ric's Food Roulette is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date below.

Contact
